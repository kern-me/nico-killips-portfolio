The Blueprint

During my tenure at Chemistry, I was tasked with managing several of BlueKC's digital presence catalog. The Blueprint was the first site I built from the ground-up.

This project was a massive undertaking and involved communicating with marketing strategists, designers, user-experience practitioners, and project managers. Within a short time frame, I converted complex business logic and detailed Figma files into a Wordpress website with customized editing experiences.
